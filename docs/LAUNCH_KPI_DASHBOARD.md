# Launch KPI Dashboard

## Activation KPIs
- Trial signups per week
- Percentage completing onboarding checklist
- Time-to-first-successful-call (median)

## Revenue KPIs
- Trial to paid conversion rate
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Failed payment rate

## Product Reliability KPIs
- Call completion success rate
- Ticket creation success rate
- OTP delivery success rate

## Retention KPIs
- Weekly active workspaces
- Tickets resolved per workspace
- Month 1 logo retention

## Review Cadence
- Daily: incident and failure-rate checks
- Weekly: funnel and conversion review
- Monthly: retention, churn, and plan mix analysis
