# Pilot Program (US SMB)

## Goal
Run 10 pilot accounts across service-heavy SMB verticals and convert at least 3 to paid subscriptions.

## Target Verticals
- Home services
- Real estate property management
- Legal intake teams
- Local logistics operators

## Pilot Structure
1. Discovery call (30 minutes)
2. Same-day setup and first call test
3. Two-week evaluation with weekly check-ins
4. Conversion review with ROI summary

## Success Metrics per Pilot
- Calls answered by AI
- Tickets created and resolved
- Missed-call reduction
- Team response-time improvement

## Exit Criteria
- Signed paid subscription OR documented reasons for non-conversion
- Product feedback entered into roadmap backlog
