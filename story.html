CallPilot AI: Product Narrative

CallPilot AI is a voice-first SaaS for US small businesses that need to capture every customer call without hiring a 24/7 call center.

Core Value

  • Answers inbound calls with a natural AI conversation.
  • Creates structured tickets and priority signals for your team.
  • Sends SMS follow-up links for photos and supporting documents.
  • Provides a web dashboard for triage, notes, and resolution tracking.

Target Launch Segment

Initial GTM focus is US service SMBs (home services, real estate, legal, and logistics) where sales cycles are short and ROI is immediate.

Positioning Statement

“Never miss a customer call again.” CallPilot turns unanswered calls into actionable tickets that your team can resolve quickly.
